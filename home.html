About Us

We are IT Society - a student-run social network connecting people from different sectors of IT through this platform. We provide contents to better equip the students with knowledge regarding certain faculty and IT skills required for the job market of today and tommorrow. From time to time, we conduct events like career counselling, talks and open virtual conferences in certain topics.

  • This platform can help skilled students get their dream job.
  • Companies can find skilled employees.
  • To connect IT field students.

Mission

Our mission is to:

  • Be to platform to discuss and share ideas/opinions.
  • Provide students with information regarding various IT perspective.
  • Be the platform for students to connect with industry at large.
  • Share guidelines from Alumni for the benifit of beginners.
  • Act as a link/bridge between job seekers and advertisers.
